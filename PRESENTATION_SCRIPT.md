# Maverick Execution Platform Presentation Script

## 1. Opening

Good morning everyone.

We are presenting **SkillTest AI: Maverick Execution Platform**, an AI-powered training execution system built to solve a much bigger problem than quiz delivery.

The problem statement is not asking for only an assessment tool.
It asks for a platform that can streamline **end-to-end training operations**:

- batch lifecycle management
- candidate and learner data management
- attendance tracking
- assessments
- trainer coordination
- feedback processes
- reminders and notifications
- dashboards and reporting

Our solution addresses all of these in one connected execution platform.

## 2. Problem Statement Breakdown

Let us first understand the real problem.

In many organizations, training execution is fragmented.

- Batch planning happens in spreadsheets.
- Attendance is tracked manually.
- Trainers coordinate through chats and emails.
- Assessments happen in separate systems.
- Feedback gets collected late or not at all.
- Managers do not get real-time visibility into what is actually happening.

Because of this fragmentation, training leaders struggle with questions like:

- Which batch is healthy and which batch is at risk?
- Who is absent repeatedly?
- Which trainer is driving stronger outcomes?
- Are learners ready for assessment or just memorizing?
- Which topics are weak across the whole batch?
- Are reminders and feedback loops happening on time?

That is the gap we decided to solve.

## 3. Our Proposed Solution

Our solution is **SkillTest AI**, positioned not as only a quiz tool, but as a **training execution hub**.

We built a single platform where managers can:

- create and manage training batches
- enroll learners
- assign trainers
- schedule sessions
- track attendance
- link assessments to batches
- monitor behavioral and performance insights
- send reminders and notifications
- collect structured feedback
- download reports

At the same time, learners can:

- see their batch information
- view upcoming sessions
- track attendance status
- attempt linked assessments
- receive reminders
- submit feedback
- monitor readiness and retention signals

## 4. What We Implemented

We implemented the platform in two connected layers.

### A. Training Operations Layer

This is the operational backbone.

- Batch lifecycle management
- Session planner
- Attendance tracker
- Trainer assignment and coordination visibility
- Notification and reminder center
- Structured learner feedback

### B. Intelligence and Assessment Layer

This is what makes our solution stand out.

- Predictive readiness scoring
- Cognitive load detection
- Panic-mode detection
- Anti-gaming or memorization detection
- Batch DNA fingerprinting
- Trainer impact scoring
- Knowledge decay tracking

So our solution handles both **execution** and **intelligence**.
